About Us

Raj Trade Concern was established in 2011 AD with the vision to bring impactful change in the educational pedagogy of Nepal. As one of the pioneers in the field of science and innovation technology, we have been passionately working to bring the best products and services to our valued customers. The support team has been continuously working for reliable after-sales support to our esteemed customers. Our long history and customer retention ratio has marked us as a successful company in the field of science and innovation. We have been able to embark on our journey from a small outlet to a bigger and wider superstore dealing with various engineering equipment (Civil, Computer, Electrical, Electronics, etc.), plant science, animal science, hospital supplies, chemicals, educational technology devices, and books related to various curriculum of study and many more. We have come a long way and we are continuously striving toward achieving success in the days to come.
